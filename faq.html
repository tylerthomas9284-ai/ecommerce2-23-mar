FAQ

Answers to the most common shopping and support questions.

Use this page to address shipping, returns, payments, wishlist behavior, and customer service expectations.

Do you ship across the United States?

Yes. Accoria is positioned for USA customers with nationwide shipping options and standard delivery estimates shown throughout the site.

What payment methods do you accept?

We support major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Shop Pay, Klarna, Afterpay, and Cash on Delivery where available.

Can I save products for later?

Yes. The storefront includes like and wishlist functionality so customers can save items and revisit them later.

How do returns work?

Eligible unworn items can be returned within the stated return window. Visit the Return Policy page for the full process and exclusions.

How do I contact customer service?

Use the Contact Us page, Help page, or Feedback page to submit your request. These support channels are also available in the site footer.
